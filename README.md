# 🚀 TikTok MarSci Australia: Measurement Insights Agent

### **The Mission**
To build world-class, automated measurement solutions that redefine the **"Time to Insight"** for our clients. We don't just process data; we bridge the gap between complex statistical outputs and strategic client challenges.

This repository serves as the engine for the **best Measurement Science team in Australia**, delivering standardised, AI-augmented, and best-in-class measurement frameworks.

---

## 🛠 Project Pillars

### 1. 📊 BLS Automation
* **Goal:** Streamline Brand Lift Study (BLS) interpretation.
* **Action:** Automated benchmarking and significance testing to instantly identify winning creative cells and vertical-specific performance delta.

### 2. 🎯 SOT Triangulation (Source of Truth)
* **Goal:** Solve the "Measurement Paradox."
* **Action:** An AI-powered engine that ingests **Incrementality (Lift)**, **MMM (Marketing Mix Modeling)**, and **Attribution** data to provide a unified, weighted "Source of Truth" for budget allocation.

### 3. 📉 Ad Decay & Frequency Insights
* **Goal:** Master the curve of creative effectiveness.
* **Action:** Automated generation of decay curves and frequency saturation points to advise clients exactly when to refresh creative and avoid ROAS erosion.

## 🤖 AI & Automation Strategy
We leverage Large Language Models (LLMs) not just for summary, but for **Expert Synthesis**:

* **Standardized Viz:** Pushing "TikTok-Brand" visuals that are agency-ready.
* **Insight Extraction:** An AI Agent layer that "pours" over raw coefficients to extract the "So What?" for non-technical stakeholders.

## 🤝 Collaborative Workflow
We operate on a **Modular Architecture** to ensure speed and stability:

* **Project Isolation:** Each project lives in its own `/projects` directory to allow simultaneous development.
* **Shared Utilities:** Common styles, TikTok branding, and math functions live in `/shared_utils`.
* **Branching Strategy:** The `main` branch remains stable and client-ready; innovation happens in feature branches.
